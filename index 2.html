Neal McAlister is a teacher, musician, and writer.

He enjoys songwriting, recording, audio engineering, and learning any instrument he can get his hands on. He is also an avid coffee drinker.

Neal is a father of one. He and his daughter enjoy feeding the ducks, employing their imaginations, going on walks, and reading Dr. Seuss.

Neal received a Bachelor of Arts in English with a minor in music from The University of Texas at Arlington in 2015. He lives in Arlington, Texas.
